## Refund Amounts

| Fee          | Amount   |
| ------------ | -------- |
| Platform Fee | **0%**   |
| You Receive  | **100%** |

Refunds have NO fees deducted. You get back exactly what you donated.

## Refund Tracking

### My Donations Tab

Shows all raids you've donated to, including:

* Active raids (still fundraising)
* Successful raids (target met)
* Refundable raids (failed, can claim)
* Claimed raids (already refunded)

### Refund Status

| Status        | Meaning                         |
| ------------- | ------------------------------- |
| **Available** | Raid failed, you can claim      |
| **Claimed**   | You already claimed your refund |
| **N/A**       | Raid still active or succeeded  |
