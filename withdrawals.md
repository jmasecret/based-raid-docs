## Lock Periods

After your target is met, there's a mandatory waiting period:

| Raid Type | Lock Period | Why?                            |
| --------- | ----------- | ------------------------------- |
| Verified  | 30 minutes  | Trusted creators, faster access |
| Standard  | 1 hour      | Protection against quick rugs   |

## Platform Fees

A platform fee is deducted from withdrawals:

| Raid Type | Fee | Example (10 SOL raised) |
| --------- | --- | ----------------------- |
| Verified  | 3%  | You receive 9.7 SOL     |
| Standard  | 5%  | You receive 9.5 SOL     |
